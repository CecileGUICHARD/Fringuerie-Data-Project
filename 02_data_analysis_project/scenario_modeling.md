# Scenario Modeling (2026 Projections)

This section documents a projection exercise included in the study presentation.

## Objective

Explore how increased opening frequency could impact the number of items sold in 2026 under different scenarios.

Examples explored:

- equivalent opening frequency scenario
- 15 opening days per month
- 20 opening days per month

## Approach

The modeling exercise was developed collaboratively in Google Colab during the study project.

The original notebook is not currently available in this repository.

This repository therefore includes:

- projection visuals
- documented assumptions
- resulting business recommendations

rather than the full modeling code.
