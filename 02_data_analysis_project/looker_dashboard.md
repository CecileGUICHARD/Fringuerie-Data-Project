# Looker Dashboard

The Looker dashboard was designed to make the analysis understandable and useful for non-technical users.

The goal was not only to display data, but to support operational decision-making for La Fringuerie.

---

## Dashboard objectives

The dashboard aimed to:

- provide an overview of activity
- track sales performance
- compare product categories
- visualize key indicators
- support recommendations for the association

---

## Suggested dashboard structure

## 1. Activity overview

Main scorecards:

- total revenue
- number of items sold
- average basket
- number of transactions

## 2. Product category analysis

Charts:

- revenue by category
- quantity sold by category
- category share in total revenue

## 3. Payment method analysis

Charts:

- sales by payment method
- revenue by payment method
- cash sales tracking

## 4. Time-based analysis

Charts:

- revenue over time
- quantity sold over time
- monthly activity trend

## 5. Operational insights

This section can highlight:

- strongest categories
- categories with high volume but lower revenue
- data quality issues
- future collection improvements

---

## Visual design principles

The dashboard should be:

- simple
- readable
- adapted to non-technical users
- focused on decision-making
- limited to a small number of useful KPIs
